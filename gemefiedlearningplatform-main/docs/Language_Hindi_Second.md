## Vyakaran

### Kaal (Tenses)
- Vartamaan, Bhoot, Bhavishya; Saral/Atikramak/Poorn.  
- Udaharan: "Main padh raha hoon" (Vartamaan Atikramak).

### Vakya-Rachna
- Saral, Sanyukt, Mishrit vakya.  
- Karta–Kriya–Karm samanvay.

### Vachya
- Kartari → Karmani: "Ladka patra likhta hai" → "Patra ladke dwara likha jata hai".

### Vakya-Roopantar
- Direct → Indirect: Sarvanam, kaal-parivartan, samay/स्थान shabdon ka badlav.

## Sahitya
- Kahani: Katha-saar, charitra-chitrank, sandesh.  
- Kavita: Vishay, alankar (upma, rupak, anupraas), chhand.  
- Natak: Drishya-vinyas, patraon ka vikas.  
- Nibandh: Vicharon ka spasht prastutikaran.

---

## Rachnatmak Lekhan
- Patra (Formal/Informal) ki rachna: Pata, dinank, sambodhan, vishay, mool-vishay, samapan.  
- Prativedan: Shirshak, byline, tathyatmak vivaran, nishkarsh.  
- Kahani: Aarambh → Sangharsh → Chudanti → Samadhan.
